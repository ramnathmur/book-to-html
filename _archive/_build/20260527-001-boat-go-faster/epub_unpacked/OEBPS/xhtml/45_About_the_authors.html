About the authors

Ben Hunt-Davis

Ben spent ten years chasing the Olympic dream. He came 6th in the Barcelona Olympics and did not even make the final in Atlanta. He scraped by on the dole and grants whilst his mates got proper jobs and started to do quite well. Ben now co-directs performance company Will It Make The Boat Go Faster? Ltd with Tom Barry. They help individuals, teams and organisations apply their 3 essential principles; Focus On What’s Important, Focus On Performance and Work Effectively With Others To Achieve Goals.

Ben maintains his involvement in World Class sport through helping organise International Rowing Events and he was also part of Team GB HQ staff in 2008 and 2012 Olympic Games.

Harriet Beveridge

Harriet is a Coach and Stand Up Comic. Harriet graduated from Oxford and joined Ernst and Young’s Management Consultancy graduate program. After working on major change programmes at companies such as Boots, NFC and Citibank she went on to specialise in leadership development. She is a Non-Executive Director of Will It Make the Boat Go Faster? Ltd and a Director of Talking Talent Ltd, an international coaching company which helps clients progress their senior female talent. Harriet has coached managers and leaders in an impressive range of organisations – from Jo Malone to HSBC, Linklaters to MTV, the NHS to 3M – helping them to unlock their potential and navigate change.
